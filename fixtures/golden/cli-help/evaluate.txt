Usage:
  claudio evaluate <candidate.mid> <reference.mid> [options]

Arguments:
  <candidate.mid>   the transcription to evaluate
  <reference.mid>   the ground-truth reference

Options:
  --onset-tolerance-ms <DOUBLE>   onset matching tolerance in ms (default 50)
  --align                         cancel a global tempo ratio before scoring
  --warp                          DTW-warp to also remove local rubato (wins over --align)

Example:
  claudio evaluate out/score.mid reference.mid --align
